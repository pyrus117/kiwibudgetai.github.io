# kiwibudgetai.github.io

Public hosting for Kiwi Budget AI's privacy policy. Linked from the app's Settings page and from the Google Play store listing.

The published policy is rendered from `index.md` at <https://pyrus117.github.io/kiwibudgetai.github.io/>.

When the policy changes, update `index.md` here and bump the "Last updated" / "Effective from" dates at the top.
